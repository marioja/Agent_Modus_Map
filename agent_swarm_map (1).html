🏗️ Architecture Overview

Hub Agents (larger cards): Catalog, Scribe, and Howler serve as central coordination points that many other agents depend on.

Critical Path (green): The main order flow from purchase to delivery represents the core revenue-generating workflow.

Entry Points: Doorbell (customer queries), Domino (orders), and Pulse (monitoring) are the primary system entry points.

Always-On Monitors: Vibe, Sentinel, and Pulse run continuously in the background.

Human Escalation Points: Compass, Gavel, and Howler can route to human teams when needed.

  • Fully Automated: Most agents run without human intervention
  • Human-in-the-Loop: Some agents require approval or can escalate
  • Advisory Only: Vibe, Sherlock, and Grease provide insights but don't take direct action
  • Can Override: Gavel, Sentinel, and Howler have authority to block or escalate
